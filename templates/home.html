About

Hey, I’m Joshua Choi, a sophomore currently attending the University of Rochester in New York. I’m pursuing a double major in computer science and digital media studies with a special interest in web development and digital art. I am a very visually-oriented person and enjoy doing graphic art and photography in my free time. I’ve been using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for a few years and love creating anything that is visually appealing. I took my first programming classes in college, and although I enjoyed it, I quickly grew bored of looking at nothing but lines of code and solving logic. There was something missing – there was no spark or passion behind the monotonous lines of code on my screen. However, I decided to take a Web Programming course last year and I loved it. I found a way to incorporate my passion for art into my code to create something I could look at and interact with. It was interesting watching different lines of code changing the layout of a website or changing how a user interacted with it. Through web design and front-end web development, I hope to incorporate both my digital art skills and my computer science knowledge to create something that people will enjoy.
