About

Hi, I’m Joshua Choi, a junior currently attending the University of Rochester in New York. I’m pursuing a double major in computer science and digital media studies with a special interest in web development and digital art. I am a very visually-oriented person and enjoy doing graphic art and photography in my free time. I’ve been using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for a few years and love creating anything that is visually appealing. I took my first programming classes in college, and although I enjoyed them, I felt that there was something missing – I found myself wanting more than just the countless lines of code on my screen. However, my view on programming completely changed when I took Web Programming, and I loved that I was able to incorporate my passion for art into my code to create something I could look at and interact with. It was exciting watching how different lines of code changed the layout of a website or the way a user could interact with it. Through web design and front-end web development, I hope to incorporate both my digital art skills and my computer science knowledge to create something that people will enjoy.
